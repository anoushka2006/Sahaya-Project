Our Values

Community

As part of Sahaya project, we are deeply committed to giving back to our local communities, specifically by working closely with local orphanages and organizations. We believe that meaningful change begins at home, and our initiatives are designed to uplift the most vulnerable members of our society. By collaborating with orphanages, we provide essential resources and support to children who need it most. Our fundraising efforts directly benefit these local causes, reinforcing our dedication to making a tangible difference in their lives. By fostering a spirit of collaboration and support, we aim to inspire others to join us in our mission to create a vibrant and resilient community that prioritizes the well-being of its children.

Children and Youth

At Sahaya, we prioritize working with children and youth because we recognize that they are the future of our communities. Supporting our younger generations is crucial to building a brighter tomorrow. We believe that investing in children today lays the foundation for a more equitable and prosperous society. By focusing on youth, we aim to empower them to become informed, compassionate, and engaged citizens who will lead our communities forward. Our commitment to this demographic reflects our belief in their potential to effect positive change and drive progress. By nurturing their growth and development, we are not just helping individuals; we are fostering a generation of leaders who will shape the future of our society.

Awareness

As part of the Sahaya project, we actively engage in raising awareness about critical issues affecting our communities. We understand that knowledge is power, and raising awareness is essential for driving change. Through our blog and small projects, we aim to inform and inspire community members about the challenges faced by vulnerable populations, particularly children in orphanages. By sharing stories, insights, and information, we hope to foster empathy and understanding among our audience. Raising awareness is vital for mobilizing support and resources, as well as encouraging community involvement. By shining a light on these important issues, we aim to inspire collective action and foster a sense of shared responsibility for creating a better future for all.

Cookies for a Claus

A Spirited Christmas Bakesale: A time for giving

The holiday season is a time for giving, sharing, and spreading joy. As high school students, we wanted to contribute to this festive atmosphere while supporting a charitable cause. That's why we decided to host an online bake sale, "Cookies for Claus," we're thrilled to share our experience with you.

The Idea
We came up with the idea of "Cookies for Claus" as a way to bring the community together and make the holiday season more special. We knew many people love baking and sharing treats during the holidays, so we created an online platform where customers could order their favourite Christmas cookies. This way, we could ensure that everyone could enjoy the festive treats without having to leave their homes.

The Products
We offered a variety of Christmas-themed cookies, including traditional sugar cookies, Santa-shaped cookies, and other festive treats. Additionally, we included brownies, snickerdoodle cookies, and cookie decorating kits in our product lineup. Each cookie was carefully crafted to ensure that they were not only delicious but also visually appealing. We wanted to make sure that every cookie was a treat to behold and a joy to eat.

The Process
We used Google Forms to create a simple and user-friendly ordering system. Customers could browse through our menu, select the cookies they wanted, and specify the quantities. This streamlined the process, allowing us to efficiently manage orders and ensure timely delivery.

The Delivery
After receiving the orders, we carefully packaged each order, ensuring that the cookies remained fresh and intact. We personally delivered the cookies to the customers, adding a personal touch to the experience. This allowed us to build a connection with our customers and make the holiday season more special for them.

The Charity
A portion of the proceeds from the sale was donated to Shishu Mandir, a local charity dedicated to providing free and holistic education to children from impoverished backgrounds. Shishu Mandir operates in 20 villages, impacting over 4,000 families and 2,518 individuals through various programs, including education, vocational training, and support for women.

Consumer Feedback
We were thrilled with the response from our customers. Many expressed appreciation for the convenience of online ordering and the personal touch of delivery. Comments included praise for the freshness and taste of the cookies, as well as the ease of ordering and receiving the treats.

Conclusion
"Cookies for Claus" was a success, and we're proud of what we accomplished. We brought the community together, shared festive treats, and supported a worthy cause. The online platform and personal delivery ensured a seamless experience for our customers and the festive atmosphere of the cookies added to the joy of the holiday season. We look forward to continuing this tradition in the years to come.

If you missed out on "Cookies for Claus" this year, don't worry. We hope to host another bake sale in the future. Stay tuned for updates and get ready to enjoy some delicious Christmas cookies with us.

Toys for Tomorrow

A community initative

In a heartwarming display of compassion and community spirit, we organized a toy drive to benefit the children of Shishu Mandir, a renowned non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to the welfare of underprivileged children. The drive, which took place both within the school and in the surrounding community, was a resounding success, collecting a substantial number of toys that will bring joy to the children of Shishu Mandir.

About Shishu Mandir
Shishu Mandir is a well-established NGO that has been working tirelessly to provide a safe and nurturing environment for underprivileged children. The organization focuses on education, healthcare, and the overall development of these children, ensuring they have the best possible start in life. Shishu Mandir operates several centers across the country, providing a range of services, including daycare, education, and vocational training.

The Toy Drive
The toy drive, initiated by three high school students, aimed to collect toys that would bring a smile to the faces of the children at Shishu Mandir. The students, driven by a desire to make a positive impact in their community, worked tirelessly to organize the event. They reached out to their school and community, spreading the word about the drive and encouraging donations.

The drive was held in the school premises and in various neighborhoods, where people were invited to donate new and gently used toys. The response was overwhelming, with many individuals and families contributing to the cause. The students worked diligently to collect and sort the toys, ensuring that every item was in good condition and suitable for the children.

The Outcome
The toy drive was a huge success, with a significant number of toys collected. The students were thrilled to see the community come together to support this noble cause. The toys will be distributed to the children at Shishu Mandir, providing them with a much-needed distraction from the challenges they face daily.

Lessons Learned
This experience taught the students valuable lessons about the importance of community service and the impact that even small actions can have on the lives of others. It also instilled in them a sense of responsibility and empathy, encouraging them to continue making a positive difference in their community.

The toy drive is a testament to the power of youth activism and community engagement. By working together, we were able to make a tangible difference in the lives of underprivileged children. We hope that this story inspires others to follow ourlead and get involved in similar initiatives, creating a ripple effect of kindness and generosity.
